About me

I received the B.S. degree in Software Engineering (Supervisor: Professor Xiaodong Duan) from College of Computer Science and Engineering, Dalian Minzu University, the M.S. degree in Computer Technology (Supervisor: Professor Guanyu Li) from Information Science and Technology College, Dalian Maritime University, and the Ph.D. degree in Control Theory and Control Engineering (Supervisor: Professors Xiaodong Liu and Xiaodong Duan) from School of Control Science and Engineering, Faculty of Electronic Information and Electrical Engineering, Dalian University of Technology in 2012, 2014, and 2018, respectively. My research interests include axiomatic fuzzy set, deep fuzzy system, clinical data mining and personality computing. I am a member of China Computer Federation (CCF).
